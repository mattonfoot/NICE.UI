Media lists

General styles for building various types of components (like blog comments, Tweets, etc) that can feature a left- or right-aligned image alongside textual content.

Basic format

The most basic media list is contains titles and optional accompnying text such as an extract or summary.

    BBC, 16 January 2015

  • NHS watchdog drafts plan to tackle A&E crisis

    ITV, 16 January 2015

  • Patients suffering major trauma need TWO nurses EACH to ensure they get the best care

    The National Institute for Health and Care Excellence also says there should be at least one nurse for every four cubicles across A&E units
    Mirror, 16 January 2015
